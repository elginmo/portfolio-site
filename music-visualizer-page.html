St. Chroma Visualizer

My DMD 1060 Final Project, A Generative Music Visualizer Based on Tyler, The Creator's Album "CHROMAKOPIA". Inspired By Old School Synthesizers and Retro Music Visualizers from the 90s/2000s. I wanted to create something that represented what it felt like to listen to "St.Chroma" by Tyler, The Creator Featuring Daniel Caesar.
